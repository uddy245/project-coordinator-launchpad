One last thing. I want you to notice something about the Jennifer Chen story I told you at the top. The lesson isn't "always log everything." The lesson is that silent watching is worse than nothing. When you can see a risk and the project can't, your awareness is misleading the project. Putting it in the log is the move that costs you almost nothing and saves the project sometimes a great deal. When in doubt, put it on the log. Get your hands on the template. See you in the workbook.
