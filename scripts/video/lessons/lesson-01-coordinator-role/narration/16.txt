The reason I started with that transcript I wrote on my first day is that it's the whole lesson in miniature. The task was "take minutes." The job was "tell us what we decided and what happens next." Everyone in that room could take dictation. The work — the thing I was actually being paid for — was the judgment about what mattered. That gap, between the task and the work, is the entire craft. Spend this program learning to live on the right side of it. See you in the workbook.
